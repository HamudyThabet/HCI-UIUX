HaBB

Our Story

Born from a passion for Middle Eastern fragrance culture and a minimalist design philosophy, HaBB bridges two worlds: the rich, smoky warmth of oud-laden traditions and the sharp, refined edge of modern perfumery.

The name HaBB /ha-bih-bih/ is a stylized, minmalist take on the Arabic word "Habibi" — meaning my beloved. It reflects the intimacy we believe every scent should evoke: personal, nostalgic, unforgettable.

Each fragrance in our boutique is curated to embody emotion, heritage, and story. Our visuals are intentionally bold yet clean — designed to match the power of what’s inside the bottle. HaBB isn’t just about smelling good. It’s about presence. Memory. Desire.

Welcome to a scent experience that speaks louder than words.

⌄

Our Mission

At HaBB, our mission is to distill identity into scent. We create fragrances that carry stories—rooted in heritage, shaped by culture, and refined through modern sensibilities. Every bottle is designed to leave a lasting impression, empowering wearers to express who they are without saying a word.

We’re here to make bold, elegant fragrances more accessible—so everyone can carry themselves with confidence, intention, and a touch of allure.

Our Vision

We envision HaBB as a defining voice in modern perfumery—where storytelling and scent go hand in hand. Our goal is to grow into a global name that represents more than fragrance: a lifestyle grounded in depth, authenticity, and understated luxury.

As we evolve, we aim to inspire a community that values both tradition and individuality, where scent becomes a signature, not just an accessory.

Brand Philosophy

Fragrance as Identity

At HaBB, we don’t sell perfumes. We bottle presence. Each fragrance is chosen to speak for you — before you even say a word.

Rooted in Heritage

Middle Eastern scent culture is the soul of HaBB. Bold, deep, unapologetic — we honor tradition through every note.

Minimalist on the Surface

Clean lines, elegant visuals. We let the scent do the heavy lifting, with design that whispers class.

No Gimmick Pricing

We don’t play games with ₱9,999 tags pretending to be less than ₱10,000. At HaBB, the price you see is the price it is. No illusions, no cheap psychology — just honest value.

Meet the Creator

Mohamed Thabet
